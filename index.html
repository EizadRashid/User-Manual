How To Print Examination Slip on myStudent Portal

Follow these steps to print examination slip on the myStudent Portal:

  1. Navigate to myStudent Portal.
  2. Click on the "Login" button.
  3. Click on the "Enter Student ID" field.
  4. Type your Student ID.
  5. Click on the "Enter Password" field.
  6. Type your IC No. / Passport.
  7. Click on the "Login" button.
  8. Click on the "Examination" tab.
  9. Click "e-Course Evaluation"
  10. Click "Click Here" to evaluate the course.
  11. Evaluate the course for all parts.
  12. Click "Submit"
  13. Click "OK"
  14. Repeat the above steps for all courses.
  15. The screen will display a green button after answering all surveys.
  16. Click "Examination Slip" option.
  17. Click "Exam Slip"
  18. Click "Print"
  19. Click "Save"
  20. Select the "Folder" and click "Save"

Note: Ensure that you have a stable internet connection while accessing the portal.
